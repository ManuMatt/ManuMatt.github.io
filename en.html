Background in automotive industry

I am already familiar with the working life. I have gained extensive work experience in various companies as a mechanic. I began my career at Teiskon Rengaspalvelu Oy in 2017. I also worked at Autio Trading Oy for one tire season. After graduating, I started a permanent position at Suomen Autohuolto Oy in 2019, initially working as a multi-brand mechanic and customer service representative. Later on, I specialised in VW and Skoda brands, further enhancing my technical knowledge in maintenance and repairs.

While working as an auto mechanic, I have developed several skills that I believe will be highly beneficial in my future job roles. Strong problem-solving being one of the key skills. The constant need to diagnose and repair complex technical issues has strengthened my critical thinking.

Secondly, precision and meticulousness have been an integral part of my car repair work. Working in an industry where carelessness can lead to costly mistakes or even hazardous situations has taught me valuable skills in error avoidance.

In addition, being systematic and organizational has been crucial in my role as an auto mechanic. Advanced organization and clear working methods have also transitioned into coding, helping me maintain clear and understandable code structures.

Finally, my experience in customer service helps me understand the needs and desires of end users. This perspective can be valuable in software projects where user-friendliness is of great importance. I have received positive feedback on my customer service skills, and in terms of personality, I am social, helpful and patient, all while maintaining a sense of humor.
